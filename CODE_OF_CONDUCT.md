# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone contributing to SSC, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues and pull requests
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Posting production secrets or credentials in public channels

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to contact@supersecurechat.com. Maintainers may remove contributions or block repeat offenders.

# SSC Group Call SFU Charter

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 2026-07-04  
**Feature:** Q.35 — Group calls

## Policy

- Mesh WebRTC capped at 8 participants (`MESH_MAX_PARTICIPANTS`).
- Larger groups may use optional SFU (mediasoup) when enabled.
- Signaling payloads are ratchet-encrypted (Engine 8.7).

*Implementation in Engine 9+ (Phase 6).*

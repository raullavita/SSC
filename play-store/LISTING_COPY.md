## Full description

SSC (Super Secure Chat) is a privacy-first messaging app for people who want encrypted conversations that disappear by default.

**Highlights**
- End-to-end encryption with libsignal (post-quantum hybrid / PQXDH) on Android
- Ephemeral by default — messages, files, and call logs auto-delete (24 hours)
- Voice and video calls (WebRTC)
- Groups, stories, reactions, and on-device translation (ML Kit)
- Security controls: app lock, 2FA, panic wipe, sealed sender options
- No browser chat — install the app to register and sign in
